Influencer Marketing Funnel

Four tiers of influencer marketing measurement.

The Influencer Marketing Funnel consists of four distinct tiers for measurement:

  1. Audience: Total following of the influencer.
  2. Impressions: Followers who actually see the post.
  3. Engagement: Likes, comments, and replies.
  4. Revenue: Sales generated directly by the post.

Source: HBR: When It Comes to Influencers, Smaller Can Be Better
