Resilience is the capacity to recover quickly from difficulties, adapt to change, and maintain well-being in the face of adversity.

Key Characteristics

  • Recovery: Bouncing back from setbacks
  • Adaptation: Adjusting to new circumstances
  • Persistence: Continuing despite obstacles
  • Growth: Learning from challenges

Building Resilience

  • Develop strong relationships and support systems
  • Maintain perspective and realistic optimism
  • Practice self-care and stress management
  • Learn from failures and setbacks
  • Focus on what you can control
