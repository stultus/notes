Character (Story Element)

Created 2025-11-23 ¬∑ Updated 2025-11-23 ¬∑ üå± seeding
storytelling concept

A strong character, typically the protagonist, is the vessel for the audience’s experience. They must evoke empathy to ensure emotional investment.

Empathy is achieved by making the character:

  • Likable
  • Humorous
  • Powerful
  • Or by placing them in jeopardy
