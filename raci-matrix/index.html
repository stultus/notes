A RACI Matrix is a responsibility assignment framework that clarifies roles and decision-making authority in projects and processes.

RACI Acronym

  • Responsible: Who does the work
  • Accountable: Who makes the final decision (only one person)
  • Consulted: Who provides input (two-way communication)
  • Informed: Who is kept updated (one-way communication)

Purpose

RACI matrices help:

  • Clarify decision-making authority
  • Prevent confusion about roles
  • Avoid bottlenecks
  • Ensure accountability
  • Improve communication
