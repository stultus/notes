An Amateur is the enthusiast who pursues her work in the spirit of love.

Etymology

In French, the word means “Lover.”

Characteristics

  • They take chances, experiment, and follow their whims
  • They are just regular people who get obsessed by something and spend a ton of time thinking out loud about it

Opposite

The opposite is Professional (note not yet created).

For Professionals

Even for Professionals, the best way to flourish is to retain an amateur’s spirit and embrace uncertainty and the unknown.
