A Digital Garden/Zettelkasten is a personal tool for thinking and writing. It has hypertextual features to make a web of thought possible.

In basic terms, it is a different format for written content on the web. Itâ€™s about moving away from blog posts ordered by dates and categories, into more of an interlinked web of notes. Blog posts are articles at a point in time, while here it is a collection of notes which are living documents. Digital gardens are also known as Zettelkasten or Second Brain.

Sometimes back when I stumbled upon Nikita Voloboev’s wiki, it became a regular hangout place immediately. But the real inspiration for me to start this space was Santhosh Thottingal. When he started his digital garden, I too wanted to try. And here we are.
